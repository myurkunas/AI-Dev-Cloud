Heinz College Admissions

This section applies to Heinz College's master's and Ph.D. admissions only. Heinz College does not charge an application fee.

How to Apply: Full-time Master's Programs. Course prerequisites and required materials vary by program, but all applicants to full-time Master's programs at Heinz College must submit the following: an online application, transcripts, letters of recommendation (2 required, 3 maximum), a professional resume, and a required essay. There is no application fee to apply to Heinz College.

International applicants may be required to also submit English Language Proficiency Exam Results and Transcript Evaluations. All applicants can also submit an optional recommended Video Interview and an optional essay.

Full-time master's programs at Heinz College include: Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence Systems Management (AIM), Master of Arts Management (MAM), Master of Entertainment Industry Management (MEIM), Master of Science in Health Care Analytics (MSHCA), Master of Information Systems Management (MISM, including the BIDA pathway), Master of Science in Information Security Policy and Management (MSISPM), and Master of Science in Public Policy and Management (MSPPM).

Part-time master's programs include: Master of Public Management (MPM), Master of Medical Management (MMM), and Master of Science in Information Technology (MSIT).

Partner Scholarships: If you are affiliated with one of Heinz's partner organizations such as Peace Corps, AmeriCorps, Teach For America or City Year, you are eligible to receive a minimum scholarship of $8,100 or $13,000 per semester.

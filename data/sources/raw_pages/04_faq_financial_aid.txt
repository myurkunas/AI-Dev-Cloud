Financial Aid FAQs

Tuition rate. Details on the cost of attendance for Heinz College programs are published on the CMU Student Financial Services graduate tuition website. Heinz College does not offer tuition waivers to master's students.

How do I apply for scholarships? The Heinz College Office of Admissions gives scholarship consideration to every admitted student who indicates, within the application for admission, that they want to be considered for merit-based scholarships. The Admissions Committee reviews the information in the application to determine each student's scholarship award. No separate scholarship application is needed unless otherwise stated in the scholarship's description. Master's students do not need to submit a financial aid application to be considered for scholarships and fellowships; these awards are made at the time of admission.

When are scholarships posted? Scholarships are posted to student accounts prior to the start of the semester for registered students and are adjusted as tuition charges change.

External scholarships. Applicants may research additional funding through resources such as CMU's Fellowships and Scholarships Office (FSO), CMU Student Financial Services external scholarship listings, and third-party scholarship search sites.

Veterans education benefits. Heinz College participates in the G.I. Bill and the Yellow Ribbon program. Eligibility for VA benefits is determined by the Department of Veterans Affairs; students provide their Certificate of Eligibility to CMU's VA Certifying Official. VA education benefits are not taxable.

Federal Work Study. Work study is a federal aid program allowing eligible students to earn money toward education expenses. To be considered, a student must be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident, be eligible for federal financial aid, and submit the FAFSA each year. Work study eligibility is indicated in the student's financial aid offer letter.

Financial wellness. CMU provides financial wellness and debt-management resources through Student Financial Services and the Financial Wellness at CMU website.

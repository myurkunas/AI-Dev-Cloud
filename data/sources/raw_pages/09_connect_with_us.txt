Connect With Us — Heinz College Events for Prospective Students

Prospective students can connect with the Heinz College admissions office, program directors, and current students on campus, on the road, or online. An events calendar lets prospective students find and register for events; events are color-coded (on-campus in red, off-campus in green, Washington, D.C. events in teal, online events in blue, and student-led events in yellow).

Virtual visits are available for each full-time master's program (AI Systems Management, Arts Management, Entertainment Industry Management, Health Care Analytics, Information Security Policy and Management, Information Systems Management, and Public Policy and Management) and for part-time programs (Information Technology Management and Public Management). Prospective students schedule a virtual visit with the admissions office through the program-specific registration links.

Prospective part-time students can also schedule on-campus appointments with admissions, financial aid, and program directors. Organizations can request a group information session by emailing hnzadmit@andrew.cmu.edu or calling 412-268-2164.

Note for prospective Ph.D. students: Heinz cannot accommodate campus or virtual visit requests for prospective Ph.D. applicants, but admitted Ph.D. students are invited to visit campus (typically late March or early April) before responding to their offer.

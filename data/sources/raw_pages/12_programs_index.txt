Heinz College Programs (Index)

Heinz College offers master's, doctoral, bachelor's, and certificate programs across two schools (the School of Information Systems & Management and the School of Public Policy & Management) plus College of Fine Arts joint-degree programs.

Full-time master's programs: Master of Science in Public Policy and Management (MSPPM), including the Data Analytics pathway (MSPPM-DA) and the Washington, D.C. pathway (MSPPM-DC); Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence Systems Management (AIM); Master of Information Systems Management (MISM), including the Business Intelligence & Data Analytics pathway (BIDA); Master of Arts Management (MAM); Master of Science in Information Security Policy & Management (MSISPM); Master of Entertainment Industry Management (MEIM); and Master of Science in Health Care Analytics & Information Technology (MSHCA).

Part-time master's programs: Master of Medical Management for Physicians (MMM); Master of Public Management (MPM); and the online Master of Science in Information Technology (MSIT), which includes IT Management, Business Intelligence & Data Analytics, and Information Security & Assurance tracks.

Doctoral programs: Ph.D. in Public Policy & Management and Ph.D. in Information Systems & Management.

Undergraduate: Bachelor of Science in Information Systems.

Certificate and executive-education programs are also offered, including Managing AI Systems and several chief-officer certificates.

Prospective applicants can filter programs by frequency (full-time or part-time), degree type, area of interest (the arts, entertainment, data analytics, technology, health care, public policy, cybersecurity, smart cities), years of work experience, programming experience, and location (Pittsburgh, Los Angeles, Washington, D.C., or online/hybrid).

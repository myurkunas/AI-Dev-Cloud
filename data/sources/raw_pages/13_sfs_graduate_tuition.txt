2026-2027 Heinz College Graduate Tuition and Fees

These are the 2026-2027 tuition and fee rates for Heinz College of Information Systems and Public Policy graduate programs. Tuition is set annually by Carnegie Mellon University Student Financial Services and is subject to change each academic year.

Master of Arts Management (MAM): Tuition $29,570 per semester; Fees $518 per semester.

Heinz College Ph.D. Programs: Tuition $29,570 per semester; Fees $518 per semester.

Master of Entertainment Industry Management (MEIM): Tuition $29,570 per semester; Fees vary.

Master of Information Systems Management (MISM): Tuition varies (charged by unit); Fees vary.

Master of Medical Management (MMM): Tuition varies; Fees vary.

Master of Public Management (MPM): Tuition $620 per unit; Fees $518 per semester.

Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence Systems Management (AIM): Tuition varies; Fees $518 per semester.

Master of Science in Health Care Analytics and Information Technology (MSHCA): Tuition $29,570 per semester; Fees $518 per semester.

Master of Science in Information Security Policy and Management (MSISPM): Tuition $29,570 per semester; Fees $518 per semester.

Master of Science in Information Technology (MSIT): Tuition $620 per unit; Fees vary.

Master of Science in Public Policy and Management (MSPPM): Tuition $29,570 per semester; Fees vary.

For programs listed as "varies," exact tuition depends on the number of units enrolled; refer to the program-specific Student Financial Services tuition page for details.

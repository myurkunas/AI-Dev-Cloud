Cost of Attendance (CMU Student Financial Services)

Cost of Attendance is an estimate of expenses while enrolled at Carnegie Mellon University. It includes direct costs such as tuition, fees, housing, and meals, as well as indirect costs such as books, transportation, and personal expenses. Indirect costs are not billed by the university, but both direct and indirect costs are considered when CMU determines financial aid offers.

Graduate tuition rates are set by each college or school (undergraduate tuition is set by the Board of Trustees and university leadership). Current tuition and fee schedules are published for the 2026-2027 and 2025-2026 academic years, with separate graduate and undergraduate pages, plus rates for visiting/non-degree students and summer sessions.

Questions about costs: prospective students can contact the Office of Admission at admission@andrew.cmu.edu; enrolled students can contact The HUB at thehub@andrew.cmu.edu.

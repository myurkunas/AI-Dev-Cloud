CMU Funding Opportunities and Fellowships (Office of Graduate and Postdoctoral Affairs)

The Office of Graduate and Postdoctoral Affairs administers some of the financial support available to graduate students, and there are several fellowship and scholarship opportunities for prospective graduate students. Information on tuition and fees is on the Student Financial Services website; prospective students should also review the Prospective Graduate Student Information page and consult their department and advisor for additional opportunities.

Funding support for current graduate students includes Conference Funding (to present at conferences in their field), Professional Engagement Funding (for conferences and other professional opportunities), and Scholarly Project Funding (small grants of $750 toward research required for a graduate degree). Stipend details vary by program and should be confirmed with the department.

Fellowship opportunities for prospective graduate students include the CMU Rales Fellows Program, which is dedicated to cultivating the next generation of STEM leaders by increasing access to graduate education. Additional resources include CMU's Fellowships and Scholarships Office (FSO), student emergency support funding, and higher-education tax benefit information.

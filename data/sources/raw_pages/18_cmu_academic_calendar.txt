CMU Academic Calendar (The HUB)

The Academic Calendar is Carnegie Mellon University's information source and planning document for important dates. It includes registration dates, class start dates, add/drop deadlines, exam dates, and more.

Doctoral course-sections follow a separate academic calendar, and Heinz College maintains its own academic calendar (distinct from the university-wide calendar). Separate calendars also exist for the Tepper School of Business graduate courses, CMU-Africa, and CMU-Qatar.

Full academic calendars are published per academic year as downloadable documents (for example, the 2025-2026 and 2026-2027 academic years), including iCal and Google Calendar formats and Pittsburgh class-meeting-day schedules. Specific term start dates and deadlines are listed in those linked calendar documents. Questions can be directed to the University Registrar's Office at cmuregistrar@andrew.cmu.edu.

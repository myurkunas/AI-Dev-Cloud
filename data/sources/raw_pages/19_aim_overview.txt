Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence Systems Management (AIM) — Program Overview

Key program information. Program length: 16 months, 3 semesters plus internship (AIM with internship), or 12 months, 3 semesters (fast track). Application deadlines: December 1 (Round 1), January 10 (Round 2), February 2 (Round 3). Degree earned: Master of Science. Internship: required for AIM with internship, not required for the AIM accelerated (fast track) option. STEM-designated: Yes.

The Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence Systems Management (AIM) program is housed in Carnegie Mellon University's Heinz College and teaches students to put AI to work for businesses and stakeholders. Every AIM student completes a capstone project, engaging directly with Heinz College's network of industry collaborators.

In the 16-month program with required internship, students work alongside industry leaders and complete an internship. In the fast-track 12-month AIM program, an internship is not required. The AIM degree is a STEM-designated degree program.

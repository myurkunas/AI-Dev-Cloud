Master of Information Systems Management (MISM) — Program Overview

Key program information. Program length: 16 months, 3 semesters plus internship. Application deadlines: December 1 (Round 1), January 10 (Round 2), February 2 (Round 3). Degree earned: Master's degree. Internship: required (a waiver can be requested based on three-plus years of prior relevant full-time work experience). STEM-designated: Yes.

The Master of Information Systems Management (MISM) program blends technical and leadership skills, with coursework in data analysis and hands-on learning through internships and capstone projects with industry clients. MISM offers two academic tracks: the Information Systems Traditional Track (16-month) and the Business Intelligence and Data Analytics (BIDA) pathway, along with optional concentrations. Heinz College is the #1 ranked Information Systems program.

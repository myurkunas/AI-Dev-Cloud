Master of Science in Information Security Policy and Management (MSISPM) — Program Overview

Key program information. Program length: 2 years, 4 semesters. Application deadlines: December 1 (Round 1), January 10 (Round 2), February 2 (Round 3). Degree earned: Master of Science. Internship: required. STEM-designated: Yes.

The Master of Science in Information Security Policy and Management (MSISPM) program is a cybersecurity master's covering digital security, physical security, insider threat, network defenses, data analytics, and management. It is taught by Heinz College faculty and practitioners from the CERT Division of CMU's Software Engineering Institute. Carnegie Mellon is designated by the NSA and Department of Homeland Security as a Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Defense, Cyber Operations, and Research.

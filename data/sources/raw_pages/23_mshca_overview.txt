Master of Science in Health Care Analytics and Information Technology (MSHCA) — Program Overview

Key program information. Program length: 2 years, 4 semesters. Application deadlines: December 1 (Round 1), January 10 (Round 2), February 2 (Round 3). Degree earned: Master of Science. Internship: required. STEM-designated: Yes.

The Master of Science in Health Care Analytics and Information Technology (MSHCA) program is an interdisciplinary program blending health care, information technology, and data analytics to improve patient outcomes and streamline health care operations. Students learn to manage and analyze health data, implement health information systems, and lead projects that improve health care delivery, preparing for roles in hospitals, research institutions, public health organizations, and the health care technology industry. Graduates earn an average starting salary of about $105,000.

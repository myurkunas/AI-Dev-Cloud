Master of Arts Management (MAM) — Program Overview

Key program information. Program length: 2 years, 4 semesters. Application deadlines: December 1 (Round 1), January 10 (Round 2), February 2 (Round 3). Degree earned: Master's degree. Internship: required. STEM-designated: No.

The Master of Arts Management (MAM) program is a joint degree program with Carnegie Mellon University's College of Fine Arts. It centers on the intersection of people, policy, and technology with an arts focus, combining courses in analytics and quantitative management with industry-centered experiential learning to prepare leaders for arts and cultural institutions. MAM students take classes with faculty from both Heinz College and the College of Fine Arts.

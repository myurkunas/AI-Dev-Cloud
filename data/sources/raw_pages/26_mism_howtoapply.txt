Master of Information Systems Management (MISM) — Application Process

The Master of Information Systems Management program has two academic tracks emphasizing multidisciplinary thinking, teamwork, and leadership through experiential learning and co-curricular programming. Some pathways have different application requirements, so applicants should review the application deadline and requirements before beginning the online application. The MISM program is a STEM-designated degree program.

Application deadlines. Round 1 deadline: December 1, 2026. Round 2 deadline: January 10, 2027. Round 3 deadline: February 1, 2027. Applicants for all three rounds of admission are given equal consideration for merit-based scholarships, competitive scholarships, and fellowship awards.

Round 1 (December 1) is best for applicants who want the earliest decision (end of January).

What do I need to submit to be considered for admission within a specific round? Students should submit their application form and the required supporting materials by the listed deadline date: essay, resume, two recommendations, official transcript or evaluation report for the bachelor's degree, and official test scores (if applicable). The Admissions Committee completes a holistic review of each application.

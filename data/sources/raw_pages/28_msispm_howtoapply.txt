Master of Science in Information Security Policy and Management (MSISPM) — Application Process

Application deadlines. Round 1 deadline: December 1, 2026. Round 2 deadline: January 10, 2027. Round 3 deadline: February 1, 2027. Applicants for all three rounds of admission are given equal consideration for merit-based scholarships, competitive scholarships, and fellowship awards. Round 1 applicants receive the earliest admission decision (by the end of January); later rounds receive decisions on a rolling basis after materials are processed.

Required application materials. Students should submit their application form and the required supporting materials by the listed deadline: the essay, résumé, two recommendations, an official transcript or evaluation report for the bachelor's degree, and official test scores (if applicable).

GRE/GMAT policy. GRE/GMAT scores are required, but applicants may qualify for a waiver by submitting evidence of prior quantitative coursework — a grade of B or higher in a college-level, credit-bearing College Algebra class (or a higher level of math). Approval of a waiver request is at the discretion of the admissions committee, which assesses applicants holistically.
